Site Name: Ibadan Wellness Connect

Slogan:"Your Gateway to Fitness, Sport & Spa"

I chose this name because it reflect the content that will be on the site which
is about creating a platform to promote fitness, sport, and spa businesses
in the historical city of Ibadan, Nigeria.

Site Purpose

The site provide an online hub for businesses operating in the fitness, sport and spa segment of the health sector, by providing online directory for its members to showcase their services and connect with potential clients. The site will also help the users to easily find and connect with these businesses offline for bookings and visitations, along with membership information form for other businesses to join.
